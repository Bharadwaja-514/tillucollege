About Tillu College

The Tillu College of Engineering and Technology was established in the year 2025, and sponsored by ABR Educational Society to promote technological education to meet the requirements of a developing nation in the context of global concern at the threshold of the twenty first century.

A team of educated, enlightened, experienced technocrats with vision, firmly determined to promote high quality education is striving to provide every facility for achieving excellence. The college is one of the best colleges in and around the Prakasam Dist. The Standards of teaching are very high when compared with the other colleges.
